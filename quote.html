Custom PC Quote Calculator

* Quotes: Once submitted, we will contact you to finalize the theme and appearance of your build before establishing the final price.

** After submitting a quote, we will review your selected build and component levels, which determine factors such as size and cooling capacity. We will then email you options for customizing the appearance of your PC, including air or liquid cooling, hardline water tubing, case type (closed, windowed, or mesh), and more.

*** Due to how often PC components get updated and the inconsistent timeframes that different componets get updated. The low, medium, and high options within the upgrade componets section reflect the following.
Base: The default specifications listed on the services page.
Low: The next step up within that segment of the build level.
Medium: Above Low but below the High/Max configuration level within the tier.
High: Maximum configuration within the build tier.
If a tier has multiple options, we will follow up via email to confirm your preferences before finalizing pricing.
